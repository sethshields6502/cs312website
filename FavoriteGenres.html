Video Game Genres

Video games come in many different genres, with each offering a unique experience. Understanding genres can help players find games they enjoy.

  • Action-Adventure

    The Action-Adventure Genre is about as straightforward as it sounds, blending together an exciting story and deep exploration.

  • First Person Shooter

    First person shooters are games that take place from a first-person perspective, and involve shooting at various enemies and enemy types.

  • Simulation

    Simulation games tend to be more realistic and can be anything from a flight simulator, a racing simulator, or even mundane tasks like a power washing simulator.
