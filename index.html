My Favorite Video Games

About This Website:

This website will be developed throughout the semester as part of CS 312. My finished website will focus on the topic of my favorite video games. There are a great many that I like, and the list can fluctuate from time to time, but I think I can narrow it down! I look forward to exploring this lifelong hobby throughout the semester!

This Website's Purpose:

This website is meant to introduce people to my favorite video games, which is a list cobbled together from a lifetime of this hobby. I hope to also clearly describe why I find them enjoyable. This is meant for anyone interested in gaming in general, or can also be used to introduce people to the hobby. With that in mind, I aim to focus my list on games that I find engaging, memorable, and most of all, fun.

Plans For the Future:

As this site expands, I will add more information about specific games I have in mind. I can also use links, images, or videos to make the site more interactive and showcase the games on this list. I may also add my favorite genres, though my existing list will likely betray my interests anyway!
